About Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is famously known as the "Pink City" for its stunning terracotta-colored buildings. Rich in history and culture, Jaipur is home to magnificent palaces, historic forts, vibrant markets, and delicious Rajasthani cuisine. From the grand Amer Fort to the iconic Hawa Mahal, the city offers a blend of royal heritage and modern charm. Visitors can explore the vibrant bazaars, taste authentic Rajasthani dishes like Dal Baati Churma, and witness traditional folk performances.
